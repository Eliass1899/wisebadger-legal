Frequently Asked Questions

How is my financial data protected?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard TLS/HTTPS connections and Supabase's encrypted database storage. We never sell, share, or use your data for advertising. In California, your financial data is classified as sensitive personal information and you have the right to limit its use. See our privacy policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Open WiseBadger, go to Settings, tap your profile card to open Profile & Security, then select Delete Account. All your data — including transactions, recurring payments, savings dens, and profile information — will be permanently removed from our servers.

Is WiseBadger free?

Yes, WiseBadger is currently free to download and use. We reserve the right to introduce paid features, subscriptions, or other monetization at any time in the future. Any new paid features will be clearly identified before purchase. See our Terms of Service for details.

Does Badger AI give real financial advice?

Badger AI provides general financial commentary and philosophical insights for educational and entertainment purposes only. It is not a licensed financial advisor. Always consult a qualified professional for advice specific to your situation.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings and tap Export Transactions to download a CSV file of your transaction history. Your data, your control.

What languages does WiseBadger support?

WiseBadger is available in English, French, Arabic, and Spanish. You can change the language in Settings.
