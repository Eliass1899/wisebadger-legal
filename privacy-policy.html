WiseBadger Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

WiseBadger ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal budgeting application. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and password. Your password is securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

When you use the app, we store the financial data you enter: expenses, recurring payments, savings dens, monthly budget, and preferred currency. This data is stored in your personal account and is not shared with anyone.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your account information is used solely to authenticate you and personalize your experience (displaying your name, syncing your data across sessions). Your financial data is used only to provide the app's budgeting features: tracking expenses, managing recurring payments, calculating budgets, and generating CSV exports at your request.

3. AI Chat Feature

WiseBadger includes an AI chatbot (Badger AI) that provides general financial commentary. When you send a message, it is processed by a third-party AI service (Anthropic Claude) to generate a response. Your chat messages are not stored permanently and are not used to train AI models. No personal financial data from your account is sent to the AI service unless you include it in your message.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your data requires authentication with your account credentials. We implement Row Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase — for authentication and database storage. Their privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic — for the AI chat feature. Their privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Frankfurter API — for real-time currency conversion rates. No personal data is sent to this service.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not serve ads. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen under Profile & Security. When you delete your account, all associated data (expenses, recurring payments, savings dens, and account information) is permanently removed from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

WiseBadger is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we store about you, correct any inaccurate data, export your data via the CSV export feature, and delete your account and all associated data at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
