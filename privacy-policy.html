Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

WiseBadger ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal budgeting application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have. By using WiseBadger you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

Account information

When you create an account we collect your email address, your chosen display name, and an encrypted (hashed) version of your password. We never store passwords in plain text. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile name from Google; we do not receive your Google password.

Financial data you enter

WiseBadger stores the financial information you choose to enter, including:

  • Expenses and income transactions (amount, category, description, date, currency)
  • Recurring payments (amount, frequency, type, due date)
  • Savings goals ("dens") and their progress
  • Monthly budget settings
  • App preferences (default currency, language, notification settings, theme)

This data is stored in your personal account and is not visible to or accessible by other users.

AI chat messages

When you send a message to Badger AI, the message content is processed by Anthropic Claude to generate a response. We may include limited contextual information about your finances (such as your monthly income, total spent, and budget remaining) so the response is relevant. Chat messages are not permanently stored on our servers and are not used to train AI models.

Information collected automatically

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or behavioral tracking SDKs. Standard server logs may temporarily record connection metadata (IP address, request timestamp) for security and debugging purposes. These logs are not used to build user profiles.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and personalize your experience
  • Provide the app's core features (transaction tracking, budgeting, savings, AI chat)
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Send you the notifications you have enabled (e.g. weekly reports, payment reminders)
  • Generate CSV exports at your request
  • Communicate with you about your account or important service updates
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

We do not use your data for advertising and we never sell your information.

3. Third-Party Services

WiseBadger uses a small number of third-party providers to operate the service. Each provider has its own privacy practices, summarized here:

Supabase

Used for authentication and database storage. Your account credentials and financial data are stored on Supabase infrastructure. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic

Provides the AI model that powers Badger AI. Messages you send to Badger are processed by Anthropic to generate responses. Privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy

Google

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google handles the authentication. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Frankfurter API

Used for real-time currency exchange rates. No personal data is sent to this service — only currency codes.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, marketing, or behavioral tracking services. WiseBadger contains no third-party advertising SDKs.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure with the following protections in place:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections
  • Data is encrypted at rest by Supabase
  • Access to your data requires authentication with your account credentials
  • Row Level Security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • API keys for third-party services are stored server-side and never embedded in the app

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you by email in accordance with applicable law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen under Profile & Security. When you delete your account:

  • All transactions, recurring payments, savings dens, and profile data are permanently removed from our active databases
  • Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days as part of our routine backup rotation
  • Server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days for security purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Export your transaction data via the CSV export feature
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for any optional processing (e.g. notifications)
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

WiseBadger is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these ages. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

WiseBadger is operated globally. Your data may be stored and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. By using the app you consent to such transfers. Where applicable, we rely on standard contractual clauses and other legal mechanisms to protect international transfers of personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. If we make material changes we will also notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of WiseBadger after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
