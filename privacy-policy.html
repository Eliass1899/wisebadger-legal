Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

WiseBadger ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal budgeting application operated by Elias Matar, based in Lebanon. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have. By using WiseBadger you agree to the practices described below.

Data Controller

Name: Elias Matar

Jurisdiction: Lebanon

Contact: WiseBadger@proton.me

1. Data We Collect

Account information

When you create an account we collect your email address, your chosen display name, and a hashed version of your password. We never see or store passwords in plain text — Supabase handles password hashing. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile name from Google; we do not receive your Google password.

Financial data you enter

WiseBadger stores the financial information you choose to enter, including:

  • Expenses and income transactions (amount, category, description, date, currency)
  • Recurring payments (amount, frequency, type, due date)
  • Savings goals ("dens") and their progress
  • Monthly budget settings
  • App preferences (default currency, language, notification settings, theme)

This data is stored in your personal account and is not visible to or accessible by other users.

Note for California residents: Under the CCPA/CPRA, financial information is classified as sensitive personal information. See Section 8 below for your specific rights regarding sensitive personal information.

AI chat messages

When you send a message to Badger AI, the message content and limited contextual information about your finances (such as your monthly income, total spent, and budget remaining) are processed by Anthropic Claude to generate a response. See Section 3 for details on how Anthropic handles this data.

Important: We recommend not entering highly sensitive personal details (such as account numbers, passwords, government ID numbers, or precise location) into Badger AI chat.

Information collected automatically

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or behavioral tracking SDKs. Standard server logs may temporarily record connection metadata (IP address, request timestamp) for security and debugging purposes. These logs are not used to build user profiles and are deleted within 90 days.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and personalize your experience
  • Provide the app's core features (transaction tracking, budgeting, savings, AI chat)
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Send you the notifications you have enabled (e.g. weekly reports, payment reminders)
  • Generate CSV exports at your request
  • Communicate with you about your account or important service updates
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

We do not use your data for advertising and we never sell your information.

3. Third-Party Services

WiseBadger uses a small number of third-party providers to operate the service. Each provider has its own privacy practices, summarized here:

Supabase

Used for authentication and database storage. Your account credentials and financial data are stored on Supabase infrastructure. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic (Claude API)

Provides the AI model that powers Badger AI. Under Anthropic's Commercial Terms of Service, Anthropic acts as a data processor, and WiseBadger acts as the data controller. Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum (DPA) and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are automatically incorporated into the API terms.

Specifically:

  • Anthropic does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models
  • Operational logs auto-delete after 7 days
  • Anthropic does not sell or share customer data
  • International data transfers are governed by SCCs (EU) and the UK IDTA (UK)

Anthropic privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy

Google

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google handles the authentication. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Frankfurter API

Used for real-time currency exchange rates. No personal data is sent to this service — only currency codes.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, marketing, or behavioral tracking services. WiseBadger contains no third-party advertising SDKs.

4. Data Storage, Security, and Breach Notification

Your data is stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure with the following protections in place:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections
  • Data is encrypted at rest by Supabase
  • Access to your data requires authentication with your account credentials
  • Row Level Security policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • API keys for third-party services are stored server-side and never embedded in the app

Breach notification: In the event of a personal data breach likely to affect your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with GDPR Article 33. Affected users will be notified by email without undue delay, in accordance with GDPR Article 34 and other applicable laws.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen, by tapping your profile card to open Profile & Security and then selecting Delete Account. When you delete your account:

  • All transactions, recurring payments, savings dens, and profile data are permanently removed from our active databases
  • Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days as part of our routine backup rotation
  • Server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days for security purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Export your transaction data via the CSV export feature
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent for any optional processing (e.g. notifications)
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Response timelines

We will respond to:

  • General inquiries within 48 hours (excluding weekends and public holidays)
  • Formal data subject requests (access, deletion, portability, rectification) within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR Article 12. This period may be extended by up to two further months where necessary, taking into account the complexity and number of requests; we will inform you of any such extension within 30 days of receipt.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at WiseBadger@proton.me.

7. GDPR (EU and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the following additional information applies:

  • Legal basis for processing: We process your data based on (a) your consent (for optional features such as notifications), (b) the performance of our contract with you (to provide the app's core features), and (c) our legitimate interests (to ensure security and prevent fraud).
  • International data transfers: Where your data is transferred outside the EEA/UK (for example to Anthropic and Supabase in the United States), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and other approved transfer mechanisms.
  • Supervisory authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. A list of EU authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu.

8. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share)
  • Right to limit the use of sensitive personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights

Sensitive personal information: Financial information (account log-in, transaction history, account numbers) is classified as sensitive personal information under the CPRA. We use this information solely to provide the budgeting features of the app. We do not use sensitive personal information to infer characteristics about you, and we do not disclose it to third parties except as described in Section 3.

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at WiseBadger@proton.me.

9. Children's Privacy

WiseBadger is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these ages. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

WiseBadger is operated globally. Your data may be stored and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside, including the United States. By using the app you consent to such transfers. Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses, the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, and other legal mechanisms to protect international transfers of personal data.
